Sometimes, the background color of the screen will change from white to a different color, like this:

Whenever the background is in color, the goal of the game is different. Now, instead of pressing the G key to every circle, you will press the X key or the M key to every circle.
Your job is to figure out when to press X and when to press M through trial and error. You will not see any squares in this part of the game.
To help you figure out when X is correct and when M is correct, you will see feedback after each press

When you are trying to figure out when to press X and when to press M , pay attention to the stripes on each circle.
Now click on "Practice" to practice this part of the game.
